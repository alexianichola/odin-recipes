Soy Sauce Pan-fried Noodles

Home

Description

You won’t believe how quick and easy it is to make these delicious Soy Sauce Pan-Fried Noodles! Savory, slightly spicy, and incredibly addictive, this dish comes together in under 15 minutes. Today’s version is vegetarian-friendly, but you can easily add chicken, pork, or beef for extra protein. These noodles are a weeknight dinner must-try — simple to make and perfect with just about anything!

Ingredients

  • Noodles cooked
  • Sugar
  • Soy Sauce
  • Dark Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Water or Vegetable Stock
  • Garlic sliced
  • Green Onion cut into 2-inch pieces
  • cups Bean Sprouts
  • Red Chili chopped

Steps

  1. Combine soy sauce, dark soy sauce, sesame oil, water, and sugar in a bowl, stirring until well mixed.
  2. Cook the noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  3. Heat oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Add garlic, the white parts of the green onion, and chopped red chili; sauté for 2–3 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add the noodles to the pan, pour in the sauce, and toss until evenly coated.
  5. Increase the heat to high, add bean sprouts, and stir-fry for 2–3 minutes.
  6. Stir in the remaining green onion and cook for another 1–2 minutes before serving.
  7. Enjoy!
